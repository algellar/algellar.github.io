d3pack

This challenge is based on the affine hidden subset sum problem[1]^{[1]}[1]. Given h,e,p\mathbf{h},\mathbf{e},ph,e,p satisfying h+se=α1x1+α1x1+⋯+α1x1(mod  p)\mathbf{h}+s\mathbf{e}=\alpha_1\mathbf{x}_1+\alpha_1\mathbf{x}_1+\cdots+\alpha_1\mathbf{x}_1(mod\; p)h+se=α1​x1​+α1​x1​+⋯+α1​x1​(modp), find sss.

The key to solving this problem is to use the concept of an orthogonal lattice. Given a lattice L⊆Zm\mathcal{L}\subseteq\mathbb{Z}^mL⊆Zm, its orthogonal lattice is defined as :

L⊥:={v∈Zm∣∀b∈L,⟨v,b⟩=0}\mathcal{L}^{\perp}:=\{\mathbf{v}\in\Z^m|\forall\mathbf{b}\in\mathcal{L},\langle\mathbf{v},\mathbf{b}\rangle=0\} L⊥:={v∈Zm∣∀b∈L,⟨v,b⟩=0}

The completion of a lattice L\mathcal{L}L is the lattice Lˉ=(L⊥)⊥\bar{\mathcal{L}}=(\mathcal{L}^{\perp})^{\perp}Lˉ=(L⊥)⊥. Clearly, L\mathcal{L}L is a sublattice of Lˉ\bar{\mathcal{L}}Lˉ.

Solving method

For this problem, we denote by Lx,e\mathcal{L}_{\mathbf{x},\mathbf{e}}Lx,e​ the lattice generated by e\mathbf{e}e and the vectors xi\mathbf{x}_ixi​, and by Lx\mathcal{L}_\mathbf{x}Lx​ the lattice generated by the vectors xi\mathbf{x}_ixi​ only.

To solve this challenge, the first step is to compute the completion lattice Lˉx\bar{\mathcal{L}}_\mathbf{x}Lˉx​.

We write h=[h1,h2,h′],e=[e1,e2,e′]\mathbf{h}=[h_1,h_2,\mathbf{h}'],\mathbf{e}=[e_1,e_2,\mathbf{e}']h=[h1​,h2​,h′],e=[e1​,e2​,e′] where h′,e′∈Zm−2\mathbf{h}',\mathbf{e}'\in\mathbb{Z}^{m-2}h′,e′∈Zm−2 ,B=[h1e1h2e2]−1B=\begin{bmatrix} h_1&e_1\\ h_2&e_2 \end{bmatrix}^{-1}B=[h1​h2​​e1​e2​​]−1, and form the lattice as follows:

L0=[pI2−[h′,e′]BIm−2]\mathcal{L}_0= \begin{bmatrix} p\mathbf{I}_2&\\ -[\mathbf{h}',\mathbf{e}']B&\mathbf{I}_{m-2} \end{bmatrix} L0​=[pI2​−[h′,e′]B​Im−2​​]

It can be proved that L0\mathcal{L}_0L0​ is the orthogonal lattice of [h,e][\mathbf{h},\mathbf{e}][h,e].

Then compute an LLL-reduced basis of L0\mathcal{L}_0L0​ and extract the first m−n−1m-n-1m−n−1 basis vectors to obtain Lx,e⊥\mathcal{L}_{\mathbf{x},\mathbf{e}}^{\perp}Lx,e⊥​.

Next, we compute Lˉx,e=(Lx,e⊥)⊥\bar{\mathcal{L}}_{\mathbf{x},\mathbf{e}}=(\mathcal{L}_{\mathbf{x},\mathbf{e}}^{\perp})^{\perp}Lˉx,e​=(Lx,e⊥​)⊥ using the BKZ algorithm[1][2]^{[1][2]}[1][2], and the first nnn basis vectors of LLL-reduced basis for Lˉx,e\bar{\mathcal{L}}_{\mathbf{x},\mathbf{e}}Lˉx,e​ is Lˉx\bar{\mathcal{L}}_\mathbf{x}Lˉx​.

Finally, we utilize the greedy algorithm described in the article[1]^{[1]}[1] to recover xi\mathbf{x}_ixi​, and then determine sss by solving linear equations over the field modulo qqq.
